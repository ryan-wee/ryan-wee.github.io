Introduction

Hi, I'm Ryan, a doctor and neuroscientist based in London.

I graduated in 2022 from the UCL MB PhD programme, and I am currently completing clinical foundation training in North London.

I am an incoming Internal Medicine and Neurology resident in the Academic Clinical Fellowship programme at King's College London. My research interests are in using basic experimental methods and clinical observations to understand the basis of maladaptive behaviours and cognitive deficits in brain disorders such as Alzheimer's disease.
